Next Event: August 15, 2020 from 7AM-9AM @ University Mall



Fueled by petrol, caffeine, and passion.

Once a month on a Saturday morning, car enthusiasts gather to drink coffee, talk cars, and make new friends. CarsCoffeeVermont is an outreach program of Vermont Auto Enthusiasts! Our next events are 7/18, 8/15, 9/19, and 10/17 from 7am - 9am at the University Mall.

Next Event

Rain or Shine

Folks will be at the event regardless of weather conditions. If we don't like the weather, wait 5 minutes.

Encourage folks to wear a mask and keep 6 ft distance from eachother. Park cars every other space in the lot.
